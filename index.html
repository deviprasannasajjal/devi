Dance Styles

Dive into the diverse world of dance. Here are some popular styles:

  • Hip-Hop: A vibrant and energetic street dance style characterized by popping, locking, and breaking.
  • Salsa: A Latin dance style full of passion, spins, and rhythm.
  • Ballet: A classical dance form showcasing grace, precision, and storytelling.
  • Contemporary: A fusion of modern and ballet styles, emphasizing emotion and fluidity.
  • Folk: Traditional dances that represent the culture and history of various regions.
